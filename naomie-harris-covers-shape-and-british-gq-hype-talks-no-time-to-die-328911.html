    Academy-award nominee Naomie Harris covers Shape magazine for October and the new digital feature British GQ Hype for November. In the latter, she speaks about her role in the upcoming James Bond movie No Time To Die. The stunning 43-year-old earned critical acclaim for her role as Paula in Moonlight, and the beauty is currently turning heads on magazine covers.

    Speaking to Shape, Naomie explained that she had a serious health condition as a child. Exactly what the condition was, she didn’t say, but she did explain that her health is extremely important to her and one of her main priorities in life. You may see Naomie’s cover photo as well as the caption Shape shared below.

    “Introducing our October cover star: #NaomieHarris. After overcoming a serious condition as a child, the star is on a mission to stay healthy and fit. She has channeled her strength into a successful movie career, one that includes athletic feats and stunt work. She played Eve Moneypenny in the James Bond films and starred in the 2017 Academy Award winning movie, Moonlight. Naomie is back on the big screen next month in @blackandbluemovie as a kick-ass cop. Despite her hectic shootings schedule, Naomie always finds time for what matters most. Tap the link in bio for how she makes her health the priority.”

    https://www.instagram.com/p/B2Rk3qeAJfn/

    In Naomie’s interview with GQ Hype, the actress dished on the upcoming James Bond film No Time To Die. The movie’s details are being kept under wrap, but Naomie did give a little insight. Naomie plays the M16 agent Eve Moneypenny first landing the role in Skyfall.

    “It’s a tie-up of Skyfall and Spectre, but with massive, massive surprises that even had me like, ‘Oh, wow!’ So I think we’re going to really shock people.”
