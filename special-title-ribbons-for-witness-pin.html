    SINGULAR RIBBON ADDED DOWN THE CENTER OF A WITNESS PIN

    Special Title Center Ribbon  - added to 1 or more of your martyrika / witness pins. The special title ribbon is a single ribbon that we add to separately purchased witness pins.  It is added down the center of the ribbon between the left and right ribbons. 

    We add these special title ribbons to existing order count of witness pins . For example, if you place an order for 30 witness pins and 5 special title ribbons, we will make 5 of the 30 witness pins with special title ribbons. 

    The special title ribbons are not for the entire witness pins - just for the center ribbon (the printing of it and the adding to an existing witness pin). 

    Typical Special Titles include: 

    - Godmother

    - Godfather

    - Mother

    - Father

    - Grandmother or Yiayia

    - Grandfather or Papou

    - Pro Yiayia or Pro Papou

    - Aunt

    - Uncle

    - Brother

    - Sister
