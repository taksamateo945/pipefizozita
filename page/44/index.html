1st major post-COVID Pre-Broadway Chicago show announced: 'Paradise Square, A New Musical'

16 Mar 2024, 00:00

CHICAGO (WLS) — “Paradise Square, A New Musical” will be the first major Pre-Broadway show to raise its curtain in Chicago after the prolonged closure of live theater due to the COVID pandemic, Broadway In Chicago announced Tuesday. Its Pre-Broadway engagement at Chicago’s James M. Nederlander Theatre will run Nov. 2 to Dec. 5. The musical is set in New York City in 1863. The Civil War raged on. Within this galvanizing story of racial harmony undone by a country at war with itself, we meet the denizens of a local saloon called Paradise Square: the indomitable Black woman who owns it; her Irish-Catholic sister-in-law and her Black minister husband; a conflicted newly arrived Irish immigrant; a fearless freedom seeker; an anti-abolitionist political boss, and a penniless songwriter trying to capture it all, the production company said.
